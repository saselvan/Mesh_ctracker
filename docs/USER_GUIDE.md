# Calorie Tracker User Guide

## Getting Started

### First Launch
When you first open the app, you'll see:
- A **Log Meal** button at the top
- A progress ring showing 0 calories
- A streak counter at the bottom

### Setting Your Goals
1. Tap the **gear icon** in the header
2. Set your daily targets:
   - **Calories** (e.g., 2000)
   - **Protein** in grams
   - **Carbs** in grams
   - **Fat** in grams
3. Tap **Save**

---

## Logging Meals

### Quick Add
1. Tap the **Log Meal** button
2. Enter the meal name (e.g., "Chicken salad")
3. Enter calories (required)
4. Optionally add protein, carbs, and fat
5. Tap **Save**

### Editing an Entry
1. Tap the **down arrow** on the Today's Meals summary to expand the list
2. Tap the **pencil icon** next to any entry
3. Make your changes
4. Tap **Save**

### Deleting an Entry
1. Expand the meal list
2. Tap the **trash icon** next to the entry
3. Confirm deletion

---

## Reading Your Progress

### The Progress Ring
- **Green fill** = On track (under goal)
- **Orange fill** = Getting close (90%+ of goal)
- **Red fill** = Over goal

The number in the center shows your current calorie total. The label below shows your daily goal.

### Macro Bars
Below the ring, three horizontal bars show your progress for:
- **P** = Protein
- **C** = Carbs
- **F** = Fat

Each bar fills as you approach your goal.

### Streaks
The flame icon at the bottom shows your current streak. Tap it to see:
- Current streak length
- Longest streak achieved
- Two-week calendar view with daily status

---

## Navigation

### Changing Dates
- Tap the **calendar icon** in the header to open the date picker
- Select any date to view or add entries for that day

### Time-Based Themes
The app automatically changes its appearance based on time of day:
- **Morning** (6am–12pm): Warm peachy tones
- **Midday** (12pm–5pm): Bright warm colors
- **Evening** (5pm–9pm): Soft lavender hues
- **Night** (9pm–6am): Dark mode

---

## Profiles (Multi-User)

### Setting Up Profiles
If multiple people share the device:
1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Tap **Manage Profiles**
3. Add a new profile with name and optional PIN
4. Each profile has separate entries, goals, and streaks

### Switching Profiles
- Tap your profile name in the header
- Select a different profile from the list

---

## Data Management

### Export Your Data
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Export Data**
3. A JSON file downloads with all your entries

### Import Data
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Import Data**
3. Select a previously exported JSON file

### Clear Data
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Clear All Data**
3. Confirm to delete all entries (this cannot be undone)

---

## Offline Use

The app works offline. All data is stored locally in your browser:
- No account required
- No data sent to servers
- Works without internet connection

**Note:** Clearing browser data will delete your calorie entries. Use Export regularly to back up your data.

---

## Installing as an App

### iOS (Safari)
1. Tap the **Share** button
2. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
3. Tap **Add**

### Android (Chrome)
1. Tap the **three dots** menu
2. Tap **Add to Home Screen** or **Install App**
3. Confirm installation

### Desktop (Chrome/Edge)
1. Look for the **install icon** in the address bar
2. Click **Install**

---

## Tips

- **Log as you eat** — it's easier than remembering later
- **Use the same meal names** for consistent tracking
- **Check streaks** for motivation on tough days
- **Export weekly** to keep a backup of your data
